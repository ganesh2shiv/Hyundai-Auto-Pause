# Privacy Policy

## Data Use

The app uses the following information from your device to function:

- User preferences configured in the app.
- Metadata about the currently playing audio stream.
- The configured volume of the connected device on Android Auto.

## Data Storage

The app stores user preferences locally on your device, all other data is used only temporarily and is never stored explicitly.

Some data may be logged to Android's system logs, but this data is not stored persistently by Android by default.

## Data Transmission

No data is transmitted off your device or to any other app on your device by the app.

If the app is installed via Google Play, some data (such as system information and error messages) may be transmitted automatically to Google Play in the event that app crashes. Please refer to [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for more information.
